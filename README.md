# Misty
Making the Invisible Impact Visible

## Inspiration
The high consumption of water utilized in AI and cloud computing data centers has become on high alert for the global world. As AI continues to grow, individuals, companies, and governments have a higher demand in utilizing their capabilities. While there are ongoing research and initiatives into alternative cooling methods to replace or reduce water usage, the implementation of these technologies remains in discussion with complications and certain environmental requirements making the application difficult.

## What it does
Our approach to this issue can directly influence individuals allowing the users to be aware and mindful of their activity. Through *Misty*, a Chrome extension that tracks the user's AI usage and reflects it into a visual experience by a teardrop character.  

###Misty’s Emotional Feedback:
* Neutral - Moderate usage (also the starting point for new users)
* Happy/ Super Happy - Low usage 
* Sad/ Super Sad - High or excessive usage 

###Misty’s Other Features:
* Happiness Level - Reflective of emotional feedback through numerical values
* Water Level Tracker - Depicts consumption of water
* Notifications - Progress and changes of user activity

## Challenges we ran into
Through the development of creating this extension, we noticed similarities to water intake apps, but addressed the differences: 

* Personal health vs. Environmental impact
* Passive vs. Manual Input 
* Awareness vs. Habit Tracking

Another challenge was estimating water usage, since exact data varies across data centers and AI systems. We decided on a generalized approximation of usage to create the feedback.
